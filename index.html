Taylor Martin

Taylor Martin makes country and Americana music with the warmth of late-70s Laurel Canyon records and the open-air honesty of a long road west.

Northbound is his first collection: mountain-light melodies, acoustic guitars, and plainspoken songs built for windows-down drives and quiet evenings.
