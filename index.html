Taylor Martin

Taylor Martin makes country and Americana music that feels like the drive home after a long day — honest, unhurried, and full of sky. Her songs find the beauty in the in-between moments that most people forget to write down.

Debut album Northbound is her first collection — warm production, clear voice, and lyrics that stick. Released through Hello Texas Records.
