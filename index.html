The Artist

Taylor Martin

Taylor Martin writes songs the way the West was meant to be lived — unhurried, honest, and with a clear view of the horizon. His sound lives in the tradition of the great 70s singer-songwriters: warm acoustic guitars, rolling melodies, and lyrics that find the universal in the personal.

Rooted in the Americana tradition of John Denver, James Taylor, and Michael Martin Murphey, Taylor brings a timeless sensibility to everything he records — music that feels like a long drive through wide-open country, windows down, no particular place to be.

Signed to Hello Texas Records. His debut single Montana Skies is out now.
